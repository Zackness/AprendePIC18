## Starting point

Use the [final partial-style project](/en/parcial/proyecto-final/) guide and [sample projects](/en/proyectos/semaforo/) as reference. Pick a system that combines **at least three course modules**.

## Deliverables

1. Pseudocode and flowchart (paper or digital).
2. Modular MPASM assembly code.
3. Proteus simulation with screenshots.
4. Hardware demo and oral explanation.
