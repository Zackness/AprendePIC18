## When to take this partial

After **courses 5 through 9** on the [study path](/en/ruta-de-estudio/#c09): [Delays](/en/retardos/), [GPIO](/en/gpio/), [Comparisons](/en/comparaciones/), and **Practice 1**.

## Real exam difficulty

| Part | Typical content |
| --- | --- |
| **I — Theory** | `MOVF REGISTRO,F` vs zero (**Z**); **C** and **Z**; pipeline; machine cycle |
| **II — Development** | `REG1 > REG2` subroutine; C/Z after `SUBWF`; **3 ms delay @ 20 MHz** |
| **III — Problems** | Pump countdown; line-following robot |
