## When to take this partial

After **courses 5, 6, and 7** on the [study path](/en/ruta-de-estudio/#c07): **delays (Topic 4)**, **register comparisons (Topic 5)**, GPIO, buttons, and **Practice 1**.

## Real exam difficulty

Partial II is not GPIO-only. The professor's model (Jun 2026) includes:

| Part | Typical content |
| --- | --- |
| **I — Theory** | `MOVF REGISTRO,F` vs zero compare (**Z** flag); **C** and **Z**; **pipeline** overlap; **machine cycle** |
| **II — Development** | Subroutine if `REG1 > REG2`; C/Z after `SUBWF`; **3 ms delay @ 20 MHz**; BRA vs GOTO; pump + BCD display |
| **III — Problems** | Pump countdown; line-following robot (`CALL CASO_n`) |
