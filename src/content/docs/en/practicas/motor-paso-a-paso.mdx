<AIExplain title="Step sequence">
Unipolar/bipolar motors need phase patterns (full step or half step). Use timers to control speed between steps.
</AIExplain>

## Typical elements

- Driver (ULN2003 or similar)
- 4 PIC outputs to coils
- Buttons for direction and speed
- Timer for step delay
