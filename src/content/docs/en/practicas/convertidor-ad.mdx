## Required steps

1. Configure ADCON1 (analog pins)
2. Configure ADCON2 (clock, format)
3. Select channel in ADCON0
4. Start conversion and wait for GO/DONE
5. Read and scale result to voltage or units

## 20 MHz crystal

Select ADCS = 101 (16 Tosc) in ADCON2 per Topic 9 ADC material.
