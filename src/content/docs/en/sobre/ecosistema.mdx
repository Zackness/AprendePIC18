**AprendePIC18** is an open-source web platform built by [zackness](https://zackness.pages.dev) that organizes the **Microcontrollers** course material from **Mechatronics Engineering** at **UNEXPO** to study the PIC18F4550 with academic rigor.

<AIExplain title="Why CALETAS?">
CALETAS groups educational resources for engineering students in Venezuela and Latin America. AprendePIC18 is part of that ecosystem at [pic18.caleta.top](https://pic18.caleta.top).
</AIExplain>

## About zackness

[zackness](https://zackness.pages.dev) is the public name of **Frank Alejandro Castillo**: software developer, **Mechatronics Engineering** student at **UNEXPO**, and musician.

He designed and built AprendePIC18 as a web platform (Astro, TypeScript, bilingual content) to navigate the Microcontrollers course with a study path, interactive exams, and lab tutorials — not to replace official course material, but to organize it.

His portfolio includes other educational and software projects such as [CALETAS](https://caleta.top), Franky+, and apps for the university community. See more at [zackness.pages.dev](https://zackness.pages.dev).

## About StartupVen

[StartupVen](https://startupven.com) is the company that **maintains and operates** AprendePIC18 within the CALETAS ecosystem: site updates, service reliability, integration with the CALETAS platform (user accounts and progress), and technical support for the resource.

Its focus is shipping educational and technology products with quality and continuity, so students and instructors can use the tool without worrying about infrastructure.

## Academic sources

Content is based on lectures, lab practices, and evaluation material from the **Microcontrollers** course in **Mechatronics Engineering** at **UNEXPO**, complemented with the PIC18F4550 datasheet and PIC18 instruction set.
